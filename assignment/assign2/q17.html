17.What are the different tags to separate sections of text?

There are several HTML tags that can be used to separate sections of text in a web page. Some commonly used HTML tags for this purpose include:

<h1>

<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<p>This is the text of section 1.</p>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<p>This is the text of section 2.</p>

<p>

<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<p>This is the text of section 1.</p>
<p>This is another paragraph of text in section 1.</p>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<p>>

<div>

<div class="section">
<h>>Heading 1</h1>
<p>This is the text of section 1.</p>
</div>
<div class="section">
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<p>This is the text of section 2.</p>
</div>

<section

<section>
<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<p>This is the text of section 1.</p>
</section>
<section>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<p>This is the text of section 2.</p>
</section>
