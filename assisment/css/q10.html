10. why should background and color be used separet properties?

There are two reasons behind this: It enhances the legibility of style sheets. The background property is a complex property in CSS, and if it is combined with color, the complexity will further increase.

The background property is used to define the background style of an element, such as a div, section, or entire part of a webpage. It allows you to set properties like background color, background image, background position, background repeat and more. Backgrounds provide a way to visually enhance and style the overall look and feel of a webpage. It can be used to create gradients, and patterns, or incorporate images that span the entire background area.

background: #FFF;
background: linear-gradient(red , green);
background-image: url("Image/path");
background-position: top;
background-repeat: no-repeat;
background-size: cover;
background-clip: border-box;
background-color: #FFF;
